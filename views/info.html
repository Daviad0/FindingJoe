Welcome to "Finding Joe"
Ever wonder where Joe runs off to during robotics meetings? Now you can help your fellow comrades in the effort of finding Joe in every room that he hides in, courtesy of the leadership team!
On the main page, login with your student ID. Then, you can see Joe's last reported location along with options to agree or disagree. If you see Joe in a different place totally, you can report his new location using the dropdown below the main box.
Joe also has the option to disable this website, but only for 10 minutes at a time. If the website is locked, you cannot see or report a sighting of Joe.
Contact David Reeves for any questions!
